Our Story

The Bishop & Co. Legacy

Founding History

Born from a Passion
for Precision

Bishop & Co. was founded in Gaborone, Botswana, with a singular vision: to bring the world's most prestigious timepieces to Southern Africa's most discerning collectors. What began as a passion for horology has grown into the region's foremost luxury watch boutique.

Our founder, inspired by decades of collecting and a deep reverence for Swiss craftsmanship, built Bishop & Co. on the belief that a truly exceptional timepiece deserves an equally exceptional retail experience. Every watch in our collection is handpicked and authenticated to the highest standards.

Today, Bishop & Co. serves individual collectors, corporate clients, and watch enthusiasts across Botswana and the broader Southern African region — offering access to iconic brands that were once only available abroad.

Craftsmanship & Heritage

The Art Behind
Every Timepiece

The watches we carry represent centuries of horological tradition. From the precise mechanical movements of Patek Philippe to the iconic sports engineering of Rolex, each brand in our collection tells a story of uncompromising craftsmanship.

Our team is deeply versed in the heritage of each watchmaker we represent. When you visit Bishop & Co., you are not merely purchasing a watch — you are investing in a legacy, a story, and a standard of excellence that transcends generations.

We partner exclusively with authorised channels to ensure that every timepiece sold carries its complete provenance, warranty documentation, and the assurance of authenticity.
